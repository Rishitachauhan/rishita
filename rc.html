Fully Funded BCA Program

Welcome to the Fully Funded BCA Program

We are excited to offer a fully funded Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program, supported by NavGurukul, Etenal University, and Shree Badrika Ashram.

Program Details

This program is designed to provide high-quality education in computer applications at no cost to eligible students. The program includes:

  • Free tuition and course materials
  • Mentorship and career guidance
  • Access to state-of-the-art facilities
    • NavGurukul is a non-profit organization focused on providing high-quality, accessible tech education to underserved communities in India. Founded in 2015 by Ganesh S and Shubham Soni, its mission is to create a more equitable tech industry by training young people from economically disadvantaged backgrounds in software development and related skills. Here are some key details about NavGurukul: 1. Curriculum: The organization offers an intensive, full-stack software development program that covers various aspects of coding and software engineering. The curriculum is designed to be industry-relevant and is often updated to reflect the latest technologies and practices. 2. Learning Model: NavGurukul uses a unique model that emphasizes peer-to-peer learning and project-based assignments. Students work on real-world projects and collaborate with each other to solve problems, which helps build both technical and soft skills. 3. Tuition and Support: The program is offered at no cost to students, making it accessible to those who might not otherwise have the opportunity to pursue tech education. NavGurukul covers all expenses, including training, living costs, and equipment. 4. Community and Network: Students are supported by a strong community of mentors and alumni. This network helps with guidance, career advice, and job placement assistance. NavGurukul also emphasizes creating a supportive environment where students can learn and grow. 5. Impact and Reach: The organization has had a significant impact on its graduates, many of whom have successfully entered the tech industry and secured jobs with leading companies. NavGurukul continues to expand its reach and aims to scale its model to serve more students across India. 6. Locations: The program is typically run in multiple locations across India, with physical centers in various cities. Some aspects of the program may also be available online, making it more accessible to students in remote areas. 7. Partnerships and Funding: NavGurukul collaborates with various partners, including tech companies, educational institutions, and philanthropic organizations, to support its mission. Funding for the program comes from a combination of donations, grants, and partnerships. Overall, NavGurukul is dedicated to breaking down barriers to tech education and creating opportunities for individuals who might not otherwise have access to such training.
